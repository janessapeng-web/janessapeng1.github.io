The Marias is a band that started in 2016.

The members consist of:

Lead singer: Maria Zardoya

Drummer: Josh Conway

Guitarist: Jesse Perlman

Keyboardist: Edward James

The Marías © Ashley Olah

albums

Their debut album is titled "Cinema," released in 2021. However they had LPs before "Cinema" named "Superclean," which has two volumes. Their latest album was released in 2024 and is called "Submarine." The most popular song in the album would be "No One Noticed."

CINEMA – The Marías

themes

The group has extensive history together and often writes about these experiences. Their songs explore themes of love, heartbreak, isolation, and self-discovery.
